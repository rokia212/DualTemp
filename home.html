DualTemp Lab Compressor

A student-engineered system that combines compressed air and controlled hot air using repurposed electronic components, designed for educational laboratory use.

Home The Problem Our Project Donate Parts

Project Overview

DualTemp addresses two challenges simultaneously: the increasing volume of electronic waste and the limited availability of affordable laboratory equipment in schools. By reusing discarded components, the project delivers a functional, safe, and educational engineering solution.

Why This Project Matters

Environmental Impact

Extends the lifecycle of electronic components that would otherwise contribute to environmental pollution.

Educational Value

Supports hands-on learning in thermodynamics, electricity, and fluid mechanics.

Engineering Practice

Demonstrates real-world system integration, safety design, and performance testing.
