A sustainable air compression system built from recycled electronic waste,
designed to reduce cost, energy consumption, and environmental impact.

The Problem

Electronic waste is one of the fastest growing waste streams in the world. Millions of electric motors, heaters, and appliances are discarded every year, despite containing fully functional components. At the same time, laboratory equipment such as air compressors remains expensive, energy-intensive, and inaccessible for many educational and research environments.

Our Solution

We transform discarded electronic components into a fully functional
dual-heater laboratory air compressor through a simple and scalable process.

Component Collection


collect discarded motors, heaters, and electronic components that would otherwise end up as waste.

Rebuild & Assemble


Components are tested, repurposed, and assembled into a dual-heater air compression system designed for laboratory use.

Test & Optimize


The system is tested for pressure, temperature, and efficiency to ensure safe and reliable performance.
